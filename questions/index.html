疑問句介紹 | Questions Introduction

什麼是疑問句？ | What are Questions?

疑問句是用來提出問題的句子，在英語中有多種形式，包括Yes/No疑問句、WH疑問句和附加問句。

Questions are sentences used to ask for information. In English, there are various forms including Yes/No questions, WH questions, and tag questions.

Yes/No Question: Is the actor handsome? | 這個演員帥嗎？

WH Question: What is he doing? | 他在做什麼？

Tag Question: You are coming, aren't you? | 你會來，對吧？

疑問句的基本結構 | Basic Structure of Questions

類型 Type 結構 Structure 例子 Example
Yes/No 疑問句 Auxiliary + Subject + ... ? Is he a teacher?
WH 疑問句 WH + Auxiliary + Subject + ... ? Where do you live?
附加問句 Statement, + Auxiliary + Subject? You like coffee, don't you?

疑問句的用法 | Uses of Questions

疑問句在英語交流中扮演著重要角色，用於獲取信息、確認事實、表達好奇等。

Questions play an important role in English communication, used to obtain information, confirm facts, express curiosity, etc.

Yes/No 疑問句 | Yes/No Questions

Yes/No 疑問句結構 | Yes/No Question Structure

Yes/No疑問句的基本結構是：Auxiliary + Subject + ... ?

The basic structure of Yes/No questions is: Auxiliary + Subject + ... ?

Question: Is the actor handsome?

Answer: Yes, he is. / No, he isn't.

Question: Were you tired yesterday?

Answer: Yes, I was. / No, I wasn't.

Question: Do you like sushi?

Answer: Yes, I do. / No, I don't.

Question: Does this machine need batteries?

Answer: Yes, it does. / No, it doesn't.

Question: Did Bob buy lunch?

Answer: Yes, he did. / No, he didn't.

Question: Have you had lunch?

Answer: Yes, I have. / No, I haven't.

不同時態的Yes/No疑問句 | Yes/No Questions in Different Tenses

時態 Tense 結構 Structure 例子 Example
現在簡單式 Do/Does + Subject + Verb...? Do you speak English?
過去簡單式 Did + Subject + Verb...? Did you see the movie?
現在進行式 Am/Is/Are + Subject + Verb-ing...? Are you studying now?
過去進行式 Was/Were + Subject + Verb-ing...? Were you sleeping when I called?
現在完成式 Have/Has + Subject + Past Participle...? Have you finished your homework?
未來式 Will + Subject + Verb...? Will you come to the party?

WH 疑問句 | WH Questions

WH 疑問句結構 | WH Question Structure

WH疑問句的基本結構是：WH + Auxiliary + Subject + ... ?

The basic structure of WH questions is: WH + Auxiliary + Subject + ... ?

例子 Example: What is he doing? | 他在做什麼？

例子 Example: When do you study? | 你什麼時候學習？

例子 Example: Where did you buy that shirt? | 你在哪裡買的那件襯衫？

例子 Example: How have you been lately? | 你最近怎麼樣？

例子 Example: Who did you call? | 你給誰打電話了？

(比較: Who called you? 誰給你打電話了？)

例子 Example: Why will you leave? | 你為什麼要離開？

WH疑問詞的用法 | Usage of WH Question Words

疑問詞 Question Word 用途 Purpose 例子 Example
What 詢問事物、活動 What is your name?
When 詢問時間 When is your birthday?
Where 詢問地點 Where do you live?
Why 詢問原因 Why are you late?
Who 詢問人（主格） Who is that man?
Whom 詢問人（受格） Whom did you meet?
Whose 詢問所有權 Whose book is this?
Which 詢問選擇 Which one do you prefer?
How 詢問方式、狀態 How are you feeling?
How many 詢問數量（可數） How many students are there?
How much 詢問數量（不可數） How much water do you need?

附加問句 | Tag Questions

附加問句結構 | Tag Question Structure

在英文中, 附加問句是指由助動詞及代名詞(代替主詞)組成的一種簡短問，附加在直述句後面(因而叫附加句)，用以尋求對方的認同、確認、亦或澄清事實。

In English, a tag question is a short question formed by an auxiliary verb and a pronoun (replacing the subject), attached after a declarative sentence (hence the name tag question), used to seek the other party's agreement, confirmation, or clarification of facts.

特點：直述句為肯定，附加句為否定；反之亦然。

Characteristic: If the declarative sentence is positive, the tag is negative; if the declarative sentence is negative, the tag is positive.

1. 肯定陳述句 + 否定附加句 | Positive Statement + Negative Tag

Tag Question: You are coming, aren't you?

Answer: Yes, I am. / No, I'm not.

Tag Question: We have finished, haven't we?

Answer: Yes, we have. / No, we haven't.

Tag Question: You like coffee, don't you?

Answer: Yes, I do. / No, I don't.

Tag Question: They will help, won't they?

Answer: Yes, they will. / No, they won't.

Tag Question: I can come, can't I?

Answer: Yes, you can. / No, you can't.

2. 否定陳述句 + 肯定附加句 | Negative Statement + Positive Tag

Tag Question: It isn't raining, is it?

Answer: Yes, it is. / No, it isn't.

Tag Question: We have never seen that, have we?

Answer: Yes, we have. / No, we haven't.

Tag Question: You don't like coffee, do you?

Answer: Yes, I do. / No, I don't.

(這裡翻譯時，需要考慮中文的表達習慣：不，我喜歡；是的，我不喜歡。)

Tag Question: They will not help, will they?

Answer: Yes, they will. / No, they won't.

特殊情況 | Special Cases

否定副詞的使用：直述句裡否定副詞的使用，如hardly，barely， seldom等，已經含有否定的意思，故附加句會用肯定式。

Use of Negative Adverbs: When negative adverbs such as hardly, barely, seldom, etc. are used in the declarative sentence, which already contain a negative meaning, the tag will be positive.

She seldom calls her parents, does she? (她很少給她父母親打電話，對嗎？)

Yes, she does. / No, she doesn't.

代名詞的確定：

直述句的主詞為名字，則大致根據性別來選he/she。如：John teaches English, doesn't he?

直述句的主詞為this/that，代名詞為it；these/those則為they。如：This is a hard decision, isn't it?

直述句為there be 句型，附加句的代名詞為there。如：There is no such thing as a free lunch, is there?

直述句的主詞為I，代名詞仍為I。

助動詞的確定：

助動詞由直述句的時態及主詞來確定，基本保持一致。

You won't tell anyone, will you?

I said that already, didn't I?

Your mum hadn't met your girlfriend before you got married, had she?

特殊情況：如果直述句裡的助動詞是am, 肯定式的附加句為am I/ ，但否定句的附加句一般為 aren't I？

I'm never on time, am I?

I'm going to receive an email from you with the details, aren't I?

注意：tag questions 附加句一般用於口語表達，寫作中，除非是敘述文裡的對話，否則很少會用到。但在口語中是很重要經常被使用的部分。

Note: Tag questions are generally used in spoken expression. In writing, unless it's dialogue in a narrative, they are rarely used. However, they are an important and frequently used part of spoken language.
