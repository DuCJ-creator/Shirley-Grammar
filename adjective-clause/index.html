形容詞子句介紹 | Relative Clauses Introduction

什麼是形容詞子句？ | What is a Relative Clause?

形容詞子句又稱關係子句，由關係代名詞(who, whom, whose, which, that)或關係副詞(when, where, why)所引導，用於修飾一個先前出現的名詞，又稱先行詞。

A relative clause, also known as an adjective clause, is introduced by relative pronouns (who, whom, whose, which, that) or relative adverbs (when, where, why) and is used to modify a previously mentioned noun, also known as an antecedent.

例子 Example: The man who is standing there is my teacher. | 站在那裡的那個男人是我的老師。

例子 Example: This is the book that I bought yesterday. | 這是我昨天買的書。

形容詞子句的基本結構 | Basic Structure of Relative Clauses

先行詞 Antecedent 關係詞 Relative Word 例子 Example
人 (person) who, whom, that The woman who lives next door is a doctor.
物 (thing) which, that The book which is on the table is mine.
所有格 (possessive) whose The student whose father is a teacher studies hard.
地點 (place) where This is the house where I was born.
時間 (time) when I remember the day when we first met.
原因 (reason) why That is the reason why I'm late.

形容詞子句的用法 | Uses of Relative Clauses

形容詞子句在句子中主要用來修飾名詞，提供更多關於該名詞的資訊。

Relative clauses are mainly used to modify nouns in a sentence, providing more information about that noun.

關係代名詞 | Relative Pronouns

who, whom, that (先行詞為人) | who, whom, that (for people)

例子 Example: She is a teacher who teaches English writing.

她是一位教英語寫作的老師。

(who 在子句中作主詞，可以用that代替，不可以省略)

例子 Example: She is a teacher whom many of her students respect.

她是一位許多學生尊敬的老師。

(whom 在子句中作受詞，可以用that 代替，也可以省略)

例子 Example: Young people who abuse their aged parents should be condemned.

虐待年邁父母的年輕人應該受到譴責。

例子 Example: A patient to whom doctors tell white lies may enjoy a longer life.

醫生對其說善意謊言的病人可能會享受更長的壽命。

whose (所有格：... 的) | whose (possessive: ...'s)

例子 Example: The students whose English grammar is weak will find English writing particularly challenging.

英語語法薄弱的學生會發現英語寫作特別具有挑戰性。

例子 Example: Her brother, whose hair is long, is a professional dancer.

她的哥哥，頭髮很長，是一名專業舞者。

例子 Example: The house whose roof is old belongs to the government.

屋頂老舊的房子屬於政府。

which, that (先行詞為物) | which, that (for things)

例子 Example: I like the dress that/which my brother brought me.

我喜歡我哥哥給我帶來的連衣裙。

例子 Example: We live in Taiwan, which has a variety of delicious foods.

我們住在台灣，那裡有各種各樣的美食。

例子 Example: I bought a new computer that is not very expensive.

我買了一台不太貴的新電腦。

關係副詞 | Relative Adverbs

where, when, why (修飾地點，時間，原因) | where, when, why (modifying place, time, reason)

例子 Example: I come from New Zealand, a country where there are more sheep than people.

我來自紐西蘭，一個羊比人多的國家。

例子 Example: They had a meal at the bar where they met each other the first time two years ago.

他們在兩年前第一次見面的酒吧吃了一頓飯。

例子 Example: I will never forget the day when I first arrived in Taipei.

我永遠不會忘記我第一次到達台北的那一天。

(that/ on which)

例子 Example: We haven't met each other since the year when we graduated from university.

自從我們大學畢業的那一年以來，我們就沒有見過面。

(that/ in which)

例子 Example: Being afraid of gaining weight is the main reason why the girl is on a diet.

害怕增重是這個女孩節食的主要原因。

(that/ for which)

關係副詞的替代用法 | Alternative Uses of Relative Adverbs

關係副詞 Relative Adverb 替代用法 Alternative 例子 Example
where preposition + which the house where I live = the house in which I live
when preposition + which the day when we met = the day on which we met
why for which the reason why I came = the reason for which I came

限定與非限定 | Restrictive vs Non-Restrictive

限定關係子句 | Restrictive Relative Clauses

限定關係子句用來限定或修飾先行詞，前面通常不加逗號(，)，含有句子的重要信息，拿掉限定關係子句，句子的意義會不完整。

Restrictive relative clauses are used to limit or modify the antecedent, usually without a comma before them, containing important information of the sentence. If the restrictive relative clause is removed, the meaning of the sentence will be incomplete.

例子 Example: I like the photo that you just posted on your FB.

我喜歡你剛剛在臉書上發布的照片。

例子 Example: The student who sits in the back of the room asks a lot of questions.

坐在教室後面的學生問了很多問題。

例子 Example: Children who are addicted to computer games will feel isolated from the real world.

沉迷於電腦遊戲的孩子會感到與現實世界隔絕。

例子 Example: People who like to cooperate with others usually have better interpersonal relationships.

喜歡與他人合作的人通常有更好的人際關係。

非限定關係子句 | Non-Restrictive Relative Clauses

非限定關係子句用來補充或附加說明先行詞, 前面通常有逗號, 關係代名詞不能是that。拿掉非限定關係子句, 句子的意義不會有重大的改變。

Non-restrictive relative clauses are used to supplement or add additional information about the antecedent, usually with a comma before them, and the relative pronoun cannot be "that". If the non-restrictive relative clause is removed, the meaning of the sentence will not change significantly.

例子 Example: Their English teacher, who just came back from the US, is sharing with them his trip to the US.

他們的英語老師，剛從美國回來，正在與他們分享他的美國之旅。

例子 Example: Jenny, whose mother is sick, has to ask for a leave from school.

珍妮，她的母親生病了，不得不向學校請假。

例子 Example: Minneapolis, which has a population of about 400,000, is the largest city in Minnesota.

明尼阿波利斯，人口約40萬，是明尼蘇達州最大的城市。

例子 Example: Andy has two sisters, both of whom are doctors.

安迪有兩個姐妹，她們都是醫生。

例子 Example: The baseball team, many of whose players come from southern Taiwan, won the championship.

這支棒球隊，其許多球員來自台灣南部，贏得了冠軍。

特殊用法 | Special Usage

as/which 引導非限定關係子句 | as/which Introducing Non-Restrictive Relative Clauses

as/which 可以引導非限定關係子句，代表前面整個句子。

as/which can introduce non-restrictive relative clauses, representing the entire preceding sentence.

例子 Example: He is very honest, as/which we can tell.

他非常誠實，這一點我們可以看出來。

as vs which 的區別：

as 引導的非限定關係子句，常有「正如，正像」的含義，並且可以放在主句前，後，或分割主句；而 which 引導的只可放在主句後，並且，當主句和子句之間存在因果關係時，關係詞只用which。

as 的例子 Examples with as:

As is known to many people, the US is a developed country.

The US is a developed country, as is known to many people.

The US, as is known to many people, is a developed country.

一些固定表達： as we can see; as is well known; as often happens;

which 的例子 Examples with which:

Andy was late for school again and again, which made his teacher very angry.

These desks are made of marble, which made them very heavy.

關係代名詞只能用that 的情況 | Cases Where Only "that" Can Be Used

先行詞有最高級修飾： This is the most interesting book that I have ever read.

先行詞同時有人和物： I saw Jenny and her dog that were running in the park.

先行詞前有first, last, next, only, very, every 等修飾： Auckland is the first city that I lived in New Zealand.

先行詞為不定代名詞時： Is there anything that you want to say?

由who, which 開頭的疑問句，為避免混淆，子句中用that：

Who is the woman that just asked the question?

Which is the book that you recommend the most?

簡化形容詞子句 | Reducing Relative Clauses

形容詞子句的簡化 | Reduction of Relative Clauses

一些關係子句可以被縮略為非限定動詞，意思不變。

Some relative clauses can be reduced to non-finite verbs without changing the meaning.

完整句子 Full Sentence: The boy who is sitting at the back is my brother.

簡化句子 Reduced Sentence: The boy sitting at the back is my brother.

坐在後面的男孩是我的兄弟。

完整句子 Full Sentence: All the books that were written by the author are popular.

簡化句子 Reduced Sentence: All the books written by the author are popular.

這位作者寫的所有書都很受歡迎。

完整句子 Full Sentence: I am a teacher who teaches English at ChungHsin School.

簡化句子 Reduced Sentence: I am a teacher teaching English at ChungHsin School.

我是一名在中興學校教英語的老師。

簡化規則 | Reduction Rules

情況 Situation 規則 Rule 例子 Example
主動語態 省略關係代名詞和be動詞，保留現在分詞 The man who is standing there → The man standing there
被動語態 省略關係代名詞和be動詞，保留過去分詞 The book that was written by him → The book written by him
有助動詞 不能簡化 The student who can speak English → 不能簡化
