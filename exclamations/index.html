感嘆句介紹 | Exclamation Introduction

什麼是感嘆句？ | What are Exclamations?

感嘆句是用來表達強烈情感的句子，如驚訝、喜悅、憤怒、讚美等。在英語中，感嘆句通常以感嘆號(!)結尾。

Exclamations are sentences used to express strong emotions such as surprise, joy, anger, admiration, etc. In English, exclamations usually end with an exclamation mark (!).

例子 Example: How beautiful the girl is! | 這個女孩多麼漂亮啊！

例子 Example: What a beautiful girl she is! | 她是一個多麼漂亮的女孩啊！

感嘆句的基本結構 | Basic Structure of Exclamations

感嘆句的兩種基本結構 | Two Basic Structures of Exclamations

How + adj./adv. + (S + V) + ⋯！

What + (a/an) + adj. + N + (S+V) + !

結構 Structure 組成 Components 例子 Example
How 結構 How + 形容詞/副詞 + (主詞 + 動詞) How beautiful she is!
What 結構 What + (a/an) + 形容詞 + 名詞 + (主詞 + 動詞) What a beautiful girl she is!

感嘆句的用法 | Uses of Exclamations

感嘆句在英語交流中扮演著重要角色，用於表達強烈的情感、讚美、驚訝等。

Exclamations play an important role in English communication, used to express strong emotions, admiration, surprise, etc.

How 感嘆句 | How Exclamations

How 感嘆句結構 | How Exclamation Structure

How 感嘆句的基本結構是：How + adj./adv. + (S + V) + ⋯！

The basic structure of How exclamations is: How + adj./adv. + (S + V) + ⋯！

How 感嘆句結構 | How Exclamation Structure

How + Adjective/Adverb + Subject + Verb + !

例子 Example: How beautiful the girl is! | 這個女孩多麼漂亮啊！

例子 Example: How slowly the snail is crawling! | 這隻蝸牛爬得多慢啊！

例子 Example: How famous Hemingway was! | 海明威多麼有名啊！

例子 Example: How good they are! | 他們多麼優秀啊！

How 感嘆句的詳細分析 | Detailed Analysis of How Exclamations

How Adjective/Adverb Subject Verb Example
How beautiful she (the girl) is How beautiful she is!
How famous Hemingway was How famous Hemingway was!
How good they (the students) are How good they are!
How slowly the snail is moving How slowly the snail is moving!

How 感嘆句的轉換 | Conversion of How Exclamations

How 感嘆句可以從普通的陳述句轉換而來。

How exclamations can be converted from ordinary declarative sentences.

陳述句 Declarative: The girl is very beautiful.

感嘆句 Exclamation: How beautiful the girl is!

陳述句 Declarative: The snail is crawling very slowly.

感嘆句 Exclamation: How slowly the snail is crawling!

陳述句 Declarative: Hemingway was very famous.

感嘆句 Exclamation: How famous Hemingway was!

What 感嘆句 | What Exclamations

What 感嘆句結構 | What Exclamation Structure

What 感嘆句的基本結構是：What + (a/an) + adj. + N + (S+V) + !

The basic structure of What exclamations is: What + (a/an) + adj. + N + (S+V) + !

What 感嘆句結構 | What Exclamation Structure

What + (a/an) + Adjective + Noun + (Subject + Verb) + !

例子 Example: What a beautiful girl she is! | 她是一個多麼漂亮的女孩啊！

例子 Example: What a famous writer Hemingway was! | 海明威是一個多麼有名的作家啊！

例子 Example: What good students they are! | 他們是多麼好的學生啊！

What 感嘆句的詳細分析 | Detailed Analysis of What Exclamations

What a/an Adjective Noun Subject Verb Example
What a beautiful girl she is What a beautiful girl she is!
What a famous writer Hemingway was What a famous writer Hemingway was!
What - good students they are What good students they are!

注意：當名詞為複數或不可數名詞時，不需要使用a/an。

Note: When the noun is plural or uncountable, a/an is not needed.

So/Such 加強語氣 | So/Such for Emphasis

So/Such 加強語氣 | So/Such for Emphasis

除了How和What感嘆句外，也可以用so和such來加強語氣，表達強烈的情感。

In addition to How and What exclamations, so and such can also be used to emphasize and express strong emotions.

例子 Example: The girl is so beautiful.

例子 Example: She is such a beautiful girl!

這個女孩非常漂亮！

例子 Example: The snail is crawling so slowly!

這隻蝸牛爬得非常慢！

So 和 Such 的用法 | Usage of So and Such

So 的結構 | So Structure

so + adjective/adverb

例子 Example: The movie was so interesting.

例子 Example: He runs so fast!

Such 的結構 | Such Structure

such + (a/an) + adjective + noun

例子 Example: It was such an interesting movie.

例子 Example: He is such a fast runner!

So/Such 與感嘆句的比較 | Comparison of So/Such and Exclamations

類型 Type 結構 Structure 例子 Example
How 感嘆句 How + adj./adv. + S + V How beautiful she is!
What 感嘆句 What + (a/an) + adj. + N + S + V What a beautiful girl she is!
So 加強語氣 S + V + so + adj./adv. She is so beautiful!
Such 加強語氣 S + V + such + (a/an) + adj. + N She is such a beautiful girl!
