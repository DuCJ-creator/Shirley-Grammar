副詞子句介紹 | Introduction

定義與功能 | Definition

副詞子句是從屬子句當副詞來使用，具有副詞的功能。它可以說明時間、地點、原因、結果、目的、讓步、條件、方式、比較等邏輯關係。

標點符號規則 Punctuation:
1. 副詞子句置於主句前時，子句後需要加逗號。
(If it rains tomorrow, the game will be postponed.)
2. 副詞子句置於主句後時，通常不加逗號。
(The game will be postponed if it rains tomorrow.)

互動識別 | Identify the Clause

When the doorbell rang, I was taking a shower.

時間副詞子句 | Time Clauses

常用連接詞 | Connectors

when / whenever
while / as
before / after
since / until / till
as soon as
no sooner...than
hardly/scarcely...when
the moment/minute

實例演示 | Examples

When: When the doorbell rang, I was taking a shower.

Whenever: Whenever I feel tired of studying, I start to play a video game.

As (隨著): As the natural environment shrinks in the face of human expansion, wild animals are left homeless.

While (當...時): While I was enjoying my dinner, my brother came home.

Before: We should take steps to conserve endangered animals before it is too late.

Since: It has been more than ten years since our family moved to this city.

The minute/moment: We violate an animal’s rights the minute we catch it for entertainment.

As soon as: As soon as the food web is broken, the whole ecosystem could be forever changed.

No sooner...than: We had no sooner started cooking than there was a power cut.

Hardly...when: We had hardly started cooking when there was a power cut.

地點副詞子句 | Place Clauses

連接詞: where, wherever, anywhere, everywhere

Where: Where there is smoke, there is fire.

Wherever: Her son follows her around wherever she goes.

Everywhere/Anywhere: You can find warm-hearted people everywhere you go.

原因副詞子句 | Reason Clauses

連接詞: because, since, as, now that, considering that

Because: She was late because she had missed the train.

Since (既然 - 雙方共知): Since you don’t feel well, you can take a rest at home.

As: As he is the only child in the three-generation family, he has always been taken good care of.

Now that (既然): Now that you are a grown-up, you should be aware of your responsibilities.

Considering that: The school requires that all freshmen live on campus, considering that this will help them study more effectively.

結果副詞子句 | Result Clauses

So...that / Such...that (如此...以至於)

So...that: His business is so successful that he has decided to open another store.

So many...that: There were so many restaurants in the area that we could not make up our mind what to eat.

So little...that: She has saved so little money that she has to give up her travel plan.

目的副詞子句 | Purpose Clauses

連接詞: so that, in order that, lest, in case, for fear that

So that / In order that (為了): The new manager extended the employees' working hours so that / in order that he could make more money.

(in order that 較為正式)

For fear that (以免/唯恐): Deforestation must be prevented for fear that creatures and plants in forests lost their natural habitats.

In case (以防萬一): Take your umbrella with you in case it may/should rain.

In the hope that: All animal experiments should be closely monitored in the hope that animal suffering might be reduced.

讓步副詞子句 | Concession Clauses

關鍵區辨 | Key Distinction

Even though vs. Even if
  • Even though (雖然 / 儘管): 表示「事實」。事情已經發生或確定是真的。
  • Even if (即使 / 就算): 表示「假設」。事情不一定發生，是假想的情況。

Although/Though (雖然 - 語氣較強): Although the exam was difficult, I did it well.

Even though (儘管 - 事實): Even though the exam was easy, I failed.

(考試很簡單是事實，但我還是考砸了)

Even if (即使 - 假設): Even if you have insurance, you will still have to pay a lot yourself.

(假設你有保險的情況)

While (雖然 - 語氣較輕): While I believe it is true, I cannot prove it.

No matter what / Whatever: No matter what he does, he always tries his best.

No matter how / However: No matter how tired I am, I can hold on.

Whoever: Whoever you are, you have to obey the rules.

條件副詞子句 | Condition Clauses

連接詞: if, unless, as long as, provided that

條件句的另一個重要特點是「假設語氣」，這部分將在第四模塊之「假設語氣」詳細講解。

If: If it rains tomorrow, the game will be postponed.

Unless (除非 = If not): You'll miss the bus unless you hurry up.

In case that: In case that there is a fire emergency, you should have your house insured.

As long as / On condition that (只要): My parents allow me to play video games as long as I finish my homework first.

Provided that (如果/只要): Provided that the animals kept in the zoo do not suffer, there seems little reason why zoos should be condemned.

方式副詞子句 | Manner Clauses

連接詞: as, as if, as though, the way

As (按照/如同): When in Rome, do as the Romans do.

The way: Dog lovers treat their dogs the way they may treat their children.

As if / As though (彷彿/好像): Some pet lovers, however, show indifference to their family members as if the members were inferior to the pets.

比較副詞子句 | Comparison Clauses

連接詞: as, than, the more...the more

The more...the more: The more we know about the wildlife, the better we will live in the future.

Just as: Animals have a right to life just as humans have a right to life.

As...as: People do not love animals as much as they claim.

Than: No one can run faster than Jacky does.

獨立分詞構句 | Absolute Construction

高級簡化技巧 | Advanced Reduction

表示時間、條件、方式、伴隨狀況等的副詞子句，可以結合「非限定動詞」，改成一種描述性的短語。

口訣： 去掉連接詞。主詞不同要保留。主動變 V-ing，被動變 P.P.，Be 動詞若是 being 可省略。

1. 時間 (Time)

When the meeting was over, all of the members went home.

The meeting being over, all of the members went home.

2. 時間 (Time)

When her work was done, she took a nap.

Her work done, she took a nap.

3. 條件 (Condition)

If time permits, I can give you a ride home.

Time permitting, I can give you a ride home.

4. 原因 (Reason)

Because there was a power cut, I had to go to bed early.

There being a power cut, I had to go to bed early.

5. 原因 (Reason)

Because the last bus was gone, we had to walk home.

The last bus gone, we had to walk home.

6. 條件 (Condition)

If nobody comes tomorrow, we will cancel the meeting.

Nobody to come tomorrow, we will cancel the meeting.
