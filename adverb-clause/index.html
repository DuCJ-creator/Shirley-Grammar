副詞子句介紹 | Adverb Clauses Introduction

什麼是副詞子句？ | What is an Adverb Clause?

副詞子句，即從屬子句當副詞來使用，具有副詞的功能，說明時間、地點、原因、結果、目的、讓步，條件、方式、比較等方面的邏輯關係。

An adverb clause is a subordinate clause that functions as an adverb, explaining logical relationships in terms of time, place, reason, result, purpose, concession, condition, manner, comparison, etc.

例子 Example: When the doorbell rang, I was taking a shower. | 當門鈴響時，我正在淋浴。

例子 Example: She was late because she had missed the train. | 她遲到了，因為她錯過了火車。

副詞子句的基本結構 | Basic Structure of Adverb Clauses

類型 Type 連接詞 Connectors 例子 Example
時間 Time when, while, as, before, after, since, until I will call you when I arrive.
地點 Place where, wherever Stay where you are.
原因 Reason because, since, as He left early because he was tired.
結果 Result so...that, such...that It was so cold that the lake froze.
目的 Purpose so that, in order that She studied hard so that she could pass the exam.
讓步 Concession although, though, even though Although it was raining, we went for a walk.
條件 Condition if, unless, provided that If it rains, we will stay home.
方式 Manner as, as if, as though He acted as if he knew everything.
比較 Comparison than, as...as She is taller than I am.

副詞子句的位置 | Position of Adverb Clauses

副詞子句置於主句前時，子句後需要加逗號。

When an adverb clause is placed before the main clause, a comma is needed after the clause.

子句在前 Clause First: If it rains tomorrow, the game will be postponed.

子句在後 Clause Last: The game will be postponed if it rains tomorrow.

時間副詞子句 | Time Clauses

時間副詞子句連接詞 | Time Clause Connectors

when, whenever, as, while, before, after, since, till, until, the minute, the moment, the second, the instant, every time, the day, as soon as, no sooner…than, hardly…when, scarcely…when

例子 Example: When the doorbell rang, I was taking a shower.

當門鈴響時，我正在淋浴。

例子 Example: Whenever I feel tired of studying, I start to play a video game.

每當我對學習感到疲倦時，我就開始玩電子遊戲。

例子 Example: As the natural environment shrinks in the face of human expansion, wild animals are left homeless.

隨著自然環境在人類擴張面前縮小，野生動物無家可歸。

例子 Example: While I was enjoying my dinner, my brother came home.

當我正在享用晚餐時，我的兄弟回家了。

例子 Example: We should take steps to conserve endangered animals before it is too late.

我們應該採取措施保護瀕危動物，以免為時已晚。

例子 Example: It has been more than ten years since our family moved to this city.

自從我們家搬到這個城市已經十多年了。

例子 Example: We violate an animal's rights the minute/the moment we catch it for entertainment.

我們捕捉動物用於娛樂的那一刻，就侵犯了動物的權利。

例子 Example: As soon as the food web is broken, the whole ecosystem could be forever changed.

一旦食物網被破壞，整個生態系統可能會永遠改變。

例子 Example: We had no sooner started cooking than there was a power cut.

我們剛開始做飯就停電了。

(No sooner had we started cooking than there was a power cut.)

例子 Example: We had hardly/scarcely started cooking when there was a power cut.

我們剛開始做飯就停電了。

(Hardly/Scarcely had we started cooking when there was a power cut.)

地點副詞子句 | Place Clauses

地點副詞子句連接詞 | Place Clause Connectors

where, wherever, anywhere, everywhere

例子 Example: Where there is smoke, there is fire.

有煙的地方就有火。/無風不起浪。

例子 Example: Her son follows her around wherever she goes.

無論她去哪裡，她的兒子都跟著她。

例子 Example: You can find warm-hearted people everywhere/anywhere you go.

無論你去哪裡，都能找到熱心的人。

原因副詞子句 | Reason Clauses

原因副詞子句連接詞 | Reason Clause Connectors

because, since, as, now that, considering that, seeing that, given that

例子 Example: She was late because she had missed the train.

她遲到了，因為她錯過了火車。

例子 Example: Since you don't feel well, you can take a rest at home.

既然你感覺不舒服，你可以在家休息。

(既然... 一般表示雙方共知的理由)

例子 Example: As he is the only child in the three-generation family, he has always been taken good care of.

由於他是三代同堂家庭中唯一的孩子，他一直受到很好的照顧。

例子 Example: Now that you are a grown-up, you should be aware of your responsibilities.

既然你已經長大了，你應該意識到自己的責任。

例子 Example: The school requires that all freshmen live on campus, considering that this will help them study more effectively.

學校要求所有新生住校，考慮到這將幫助他們更有效地學習。

結果副詞子句 | Result Clauses

結果副詞子句連接詞 | Result Clause Connectors

so…that, such…that

例子 Example: His business is so successful that he has decided to open another store.

他的生意非常成功，以至於他決定開另一家店。

(such a success that he has decided to open another store.)

例子 Example: There were so many restaurants in the area that we could not make up our mind what to eat.

該地區有如此多的餐廳，以至於我們無法決定吃什麼。

例子 Example: She has saved so little money that she has to give up her travel plan.

她存了這麼少的錢，以至於她不得不放棄她的旅行計劃。

目的副詞子句 | Purpose Clauses

目的副詞子句連接詞 | Purpose Clause Connectors

so that; in order that, lest, in case, for fear that (以防), in the hope that

例子 Example: The new manager extended the employees' working hours so that / in order that he could make more money.

新經理延長了員工的工作時間，以便他能賺更多的錢。

(in order that 較為正式)

例子 Example: Deforestation must be prevented for fear that creatures and plants in forests lost their natural habitats.

必須防止森林砍伐，以免森林中的生物和植物失去其自然棲息地。

例子 Example: Take your umbrella with you in case it may/should rain.

帶上你的雨傘，以防下雨。

例子 Example: All animal experiments should be closely monitored in the hope that animal suffering might be reduced.

所有動物實驗都應受到密切監控，以期減少動物的痛苦。

讓步副詞子句 | Concession Clauses

讓步副詞子句連接詞 | Concession Clause Connectors

though/although, even though, even if；no matter…; in spite of the fact that, while, whatever, whoever, however, whichever

例子 Example: Though/Although the exam was difficult, I did it well.

儘管考試很難，我還是考得很好。

(儘管)

例子 Example: Even though the exam was easy, I failed.

儘管考試很容易，我還是沒通過。

(儘管)

例子 Example: While I believe it is true, I cannot prove it.

雖然我相信這是真的，但我無法證明。

(儘管，雖然。語氣較輕)

例子 Example: Even if you have insurance, you will still have to pay a lot yourself.

即使你有保險，你仍然需要自己支付很多。

(就算，即使)

例子 Example: Whatever/ No matter what he does, he always tries his best.

無論他做什麼，他總是盡力而為。

(無論做什麼)

例子 Example: However / No matter how tired I am, I can hold on.

無論我多累，我都能堅持。

(無論多累)

例子 Example: Whoever you are, you have to obey the rules.

無論你是誰，你都必須遵守規則。

條件副詞子句 | Condition Clauses

條件副詞子句連接詞 | Condition Clause Connectors

if, unless, as/so long as, only if, providing/provided that, suppose that, in case that, on condition that

例子 Example: If it rains tomorrow, the game will be postponed.

如果明天下雨，比賽將被推遲。

例子 Example: You'll miss the bus unless you hurry up.

除非你趕快，否則你會錯過巴士。

例子 Example: In case that there is a fire emergency, you should have your house insured.

萬一發生火災緊急情況，你應該為你的房子投保。

例子 Example: My parents allow me to play video games as long as /on (the) condition that I finish my homework first.

只要我先完成作業，我的父母就允許我玩電子遊戲。

例子 Example: Provided that the animals kept in the zoo do not suffer, there seems little reason why zoos should be condemned.

只要關在動物園裡的動物不受苦，似乎沒有什麼理由譴責動物園。

注意：條件句的另一個重要特點是「假設語氣」。具體請看第四模塊之「假設語氣」。

Note: Another important feature of conditional sentences is the "subjunctive mood". Please refer to the "Subjunctive Mood" section in Module IV for details.

方式副詞子句 | Manner Clauses

方式副詞子句連接詞 | Manner Clause Connectors

as, as if, as though, how, the way

例子 Example: When in Rome, do as the Roman do.

入鄉隨俗。

例子 Example: Dog lovers treat their dogs the way they may treat their children.

愛狗人士對待他們的狗就像對待他們的孩子一樣。

例子 Example: Some pet lovers, however, show indifference to their family members as if (as though) the members were inferior to the pets.

然而，一些寵物愛好者對他們的家人表現出冷漠，彷彿家人不如寵物。

(彷彿，好像)

比較副詞子句 | Comparison Clauses

比較副詞子句連接詞 | Comparison Clause Connectors

as, than, the more…the more; as…as, not so…as; no…more than

例子 Example: The more we know about the wildlife, the better we will live in the future.

我們對野生動物的了解越多，我們將來的生活就會越好。

例子 Example: Animals have a right to life just as humans have a right to life.

動物有生存的權利，就像人類有生存的權利一樣。

例子 Example: People do not love animals as much as they claim.

人們並不像他們聲稱的那樣愛動物。

例子 Example: No one can run faster than Jacky does.

沒有人能跑得比傑基快。

獨立分詞構句 | Absolute Construction

獨立分詞構句 | Absolute Construction

表示時間、條件、方式、伴隨狀況等的副詞子句，可以被改成一種叫「獨立分詞構句」的描述性的短語(不是句子)。

Adverb clauses expressing time, condition, manner, accompanying circumstances, etc., can be changed into a descriptive phrase called an "absolute construction" (not a sentence).

獨立分詞構句 Absolute Construction: The meeting being over, all of the members went home.

副詞子句 Adverb Clause: When the meeting was over, all of the members went home.

會議結束，所有的成員都回家了。

獨立分詞構句 Absolute Construction: Her work done, she took a nap.

副詞子句 Adverb Clause: When her work was done, she took a nap.

工作做完了，她小睡了一下。

獨立分詞構句 Absolute Construction: Time permitting, I can give you a ride home.

副詞子句 Adverb Clause: If time permits, I can give you a ride home.

時間允許的話，我可以送你回家。

獨立分詞構句 Absolute Construction: There being a power cut, I had to go to bed early.

副詞子句 Adverb Clause: Because there was a power cut, I had to go to bed early.

停電了，我只好早睡。

獨立分詞構句 Absolute Construction: The last bus gone, we had to walk home.

副詞子句 Adverb Clause: Because the last bus was gone, we had to walk home.

最後一班巴士走了，我們只能走路回家。

獨立分詞構句 Absolute Construction: Nobody to come tomorrow, we will cancel the meeting.

副詞子句 Adverb Clause: If nobody comes tomorrow, we will cancel the meeting.

如果明天沒有人來，我們就取消會議。
