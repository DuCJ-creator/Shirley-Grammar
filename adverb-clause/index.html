副詞子句介紹 | Introduction

什麼是副詞子句？ | What is an Adverb Clause?

副詞子句，即從屬子句當副詞來使用，具有副詞的功能，說明時間、地點、原因、結果、目的、讓步，條件、方式、比較等方面的邏輯關係。

例子 Example: When the doorbell rang, I was taking a shower.

當門鈴響時，我正在淋浴。

例子 Example: She was late because she had missed the train.

她遲到了，因為她錯過了火車。

副詞子句的基本結構 | Basic Structure

位置規則 Position Rule:
1. 子句在前，後面要加逗號 (If it rains, we stay home.)
2. 子句在後，通常不加逗號 (We stay home if it rains.)
類型 Type 連接詞 Connectors 例子 Example
時間 Time when, while, as, before... I will call you when I arrive.
原因 Reason because, since, as... He left early because he was tired.
條件 Condition if, unless... If it rains, we will stay home.
讓步 Concession although, though... Although it rained, we went out.

互動識別 | Identify the Clause

When the doorbell rang, I was taking a shower.

時間副詞子句 | Time Clauses

常見連接詞 | Common Connectors

when 當...時
while 當...時(持續)
before 在...之前
after 在...之後
since 自從
until 直到
as soon as 一...就...
the moment 一...就...

Since (自從): It has been ten years since we moved here.

As soon as (一...就...): As soon as the food web is broken, the ecosystem changes.

No sooner...than (剛...就...): We had no sooner started cooking than the power went out.

句型演練 | Practice

______ I feel tired, I take a break.

地點副詞子句 | Place Clauses

連接詞: Where, Wherever

Where: Where there is smoke, there is fire.

(無風不起浪 / 有煙的地方就有火)

Wherever: Her son follows her wherever she goes.

句型演練 | Practice

I will follow you ______ you go.

原因副詞子句 | Reason Clauses

常見連接詞 | Common Connectors

because 因為
since 既然/因為
as 由於
now that 既然

Because: She was late because she missed the train.

Since (既然): Since you don't feel well, you should rest.

註：Since 表示雙方已知的理由。

Now that (既然): Now that you are a grown-up, act like one.

句型演練 | Practice

______ you are here, let's start.

結果副詞子句 | Result Clauses

So...that / Such...that (如此...以至於)

So + Adj/Adv + that:

It was so cold that the lake froze.

(天氣太冷了，以至於湖水結冰了)

Such + Noun + that:

It was such a cold day that the lake froze.

句型演練 | Practice

It was ______ cold ______ the lake froze.

目的副詞子句 | Purpose Clauses

為了 / 以免

so that 為了
in order that 為了
lest 以免
for fear that 唯恐

So that: She studied hard so that she could pass the exam.

In case (以防萬一): Take an umbrella in case it rains.

句型演練 | Practice

She studied hard ______ she could pass.

讓步副詞子句 | Concession Clauses

雖然 / 即使 / 無論

Although/Though (雖然): Although it was raining, we went for a walk.

Even if (即使 - 假設): Even if you have insurance, you still have to pay.

No matter wh- (無論): No matter what he does, he tries his best.

句型演練 | Practice

______ it was raining, we went out.

條件副詞子句 | Condition Clauses

如果 / 除非 / 只要

If (如果): If it rains tomorrow, the game will be canceled.

Unless (除非 = If not): You'll miss the bus unless you hurry.

As long as (只要): You can play as long as you finish homework.

句型演練 | Practice

______ it rains, we stay home.

方式副詞子句 | Manner Clauses

如同 / 彷彿

As (按照/如同): Do in Rome as the Romans do.

As if (彷彿): He acted as if he knew everything.

句型演練 | Practice

He acted ______ he knew everything.

比較副詞子句 | Comparison Clauses

比... / 越...越...

The more...the more: The more we know, the better we live.

Than: She is taller than I am.

句型演練 | Practice

She is taller ______ I am.

獨立分詞構句 | Absolute Construction

高級簡化技巧 | Advanced Reduction

當主詞不同時，保留主詞，將動詞改為分詞 (V-ing 或 P.P.)。

口訣： 連接詞去掉，主詞不同要保留，主動 V-ing，被動 P.P.，Be 動詞若是 being 可省略。

原句: Because there was a power cut, I went to bed.

簡化: There being a power cut, I went to bed.

原句: If time permits, I will come.

簡化: Time permitting, I will come.

原句: When her work was done, she took a nap.

簡化: Her work done, she took a nap.

變身練習 | Transformation

When the meeting was over, we went home.
