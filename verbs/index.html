動詞種類 | Verb Types

連綴動詞 | Linking Verbs

連綴動詞不表示動作，而是連接主語和描述主語的詞。

Linking verbs do not show action but connect the subject to a word that describes it.

例子 Example: She is a teacher. | 她是一名老師。

例子 Example: He feels happy. | 他感到快樂。

及物動詞和不及物動詞 | Transitive and Intransitive Verbs

及物動詞 (Transitive verbs) 需要接受詞。

不及物動詞 (Intransitive verbs) 不需要接受詞。

及物 Transitive: She reads a book. | 她讀一本書。

不及物 Intransitive: He sleeps. | 他睡覺。

情態助動詞 | Modal Auxiliary Verbs

情態助動詞後面接動詞原形，不受主語影響。

Modal auxiliary verbs are followed by the base form of the verb and are not affected by the subject.

單詞 Word 意思 Meaning 例子 Example
can 能力; 允許; 請求; 可能性 I can speak three languages. (能力)
could 能力; 請求; 猜測 Could you open the window? (禮貌請求)
may 請求、允許; 祝願; 猜測 May I open the window? (請求)
might 可能; 請求 You might be disappointed. (可能)
must 必須; 命令; 猜測 Students must do homework. (義務)
ought to 應該/應當; 可能性 You ought to work harder next year. (應當)
shall 建議; 將/會 Shall we leave now? (詢問意見)
should 應該/該 You should finish the task on time. (責任)
will 未來可能/意願 I will be away for a week.
would 希望/邀請 Would you like to go skating with us?

助動詞 | Auxiliary Verbs

幫助主要動詞構成各種時態、語態、句型(如疑問句否定句)，沒有實際語意。

Auxiliary verbs help main verbs form various tenses, voices, and sentence patterns (such as questions and negatives), and have no actual meaning.

助動詞 Auxiliary Verb 用法 Usage 例子 Example
be (am/is/are/was/were) 用於進行式和被動語態 She is studying. (進行式)
The car was repaired. (被動語態)
do/does/did 用於簡單式疑問句和否定句 Do you like coffee? (疑問句)
He doesn't live here. (否定句)
have/has/had 用於完成式 I have finished my work. (現在完成式)
She had left before I arrived. (過去完成式)

時態 | Tenses

時態概述 | Tense Overview

英語動詞時態表示動作發生的時間。

English verb tenses indicate the time when an action occurs.

簡單時態 | Simple Tenses

時態 Tense 用法 Usage 例子 Example
現在簡單式 Present Simple 習慣、事實、常規 She works every day.
過去簡單式 Past Simple 過去完成的動作 He visited Paris last year.
未來簡單式 Future Simple 未來的動作或預測 They will arrive tomorrow.

進行時態 | Continuous Tenses

時態 Tense 用法 Usage 例子 Example
現在進行式 Present Continuous 現在正在進行的動作 I am studying now.
過去進行式 Past Continuous 過去某一時刻正在進行的動作 She was cooking when I called.
未來進行式 Future Continuous 未來某一時刻將進行的動作 They will be traveling this time next week.

完成時態 | Perfect Tenses

時態 Tense 用法 Usage 例子 Example
現在完成式 Present Perfect 過去發生但與現在有關的動作 I have finished my homework.
過去完成式 Past Perfect 過去某時之前已完成的動作 She had left before I arrived.
未來完成式 Future Perfect 未來某時之前將完成的動作 By next year, I will have graduated.

完成進行時態 | Perfect Continuous Tenses

時態 Tense 用法 Usage 例子 Example
現在完成進行式 Present Perfect Continuous 從過去持續到現在的動作 I have been studying for two hours.
過去完成進行式 Past Perfect Continuous 過去某時之前持續的動作 She had been waiting for an hour when he arrived.
未來完成進行式 Future Perfect Continuous 未來某時之前將持續的動作 By next month, I will have been working here for five years.

語態 | Voices

主動語態 | Active Voice

主語執行動作。

The subject performs the action.

例子 Example: The cat chased the mouse. | 貓追老鼠。

被動語態 | Passive Voice

主語接受動作。基本構成是 be + 過去分詞。

The subject receives the action. Basic structure is be + past participle.

例子 Example: The mouse was chased by the cat. | 老鼠被貓追。

時態 Tense 被動結構 Passive Structure 例子 Example
現在簡單式 Present Simple am/is/are + past participle The car is not locked.
過去簡單式 Past Simple was/were + past participle My purse was stolen.
未來簡單式 Future Simple will be + past participle The meeting will be held next Wednesday.
現在進行式 Present Continuous am/is/are being + past participle The meeting is being held now.
現在完成式 Present Perfect has/have been + past participle The flight has been canceled due to bad weather.
過去完成式 Past Perfect had been + past participle The flight had been canceled before the flight crew were ready.

重點內容 | Key Points

動詞的重要性 | Importance of Verbs

動詞是英語學習中的重中之重。五種基本句型中，動詞都是核心。寫作時也一定要先釐清每一個句子的主動詞。

Verbs are the most important part of English learning. In the five basic sentence patterns, verbs are the core. When writing, it is essential to clarify the main verb of each sentence first.

時態和語態的規律 | Patterns of Tenses and Voices

主動詞有時態和語態的變化，但時態和語態都是有規律的，記住規律，多加練習。

Main verbs change in tense and voice, but these changes follow patterns. Remember the patterns and practice more.

動詞片語 | Phrasal Verbs

動詞還會跟各種介詞搭配，形成不同的動詞片語。如：put up, take in等，需要積累及記憶。

Verbs also combine with various prepositions to form different phrasal verbs. For example: put up, take in, etc. These need to be accumulated and memorized.

重要提醒 | Important Reminders

  • 現在簡單式要注意動詞的第三人稱單數形式，如：She likes jogging.
  • 過去簡單式要注意過去式的不規則變化。
  • 完成時態要注意使用動詞的過去分詞，並注意過去分詞的不規則變化。
  • 助動詞 do, does, did, have, has, had 本身也可以做主要動詞，如：What do you do? I have had lunch 等。不要混淆。
  • 未來式中，will 一般表示臨時起意，而be going to 表示計畫好的事。如：It's cold; I'll close the window. I am going to buy a new phone.
  • 被動語態需要和 be + -ed 形容詞區分開, 如: I am excited. 不是被動語態。
