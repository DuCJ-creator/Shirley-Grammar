現在分詞介紹 | Present Participle Introduction

什麼是現在分詞？ | What is a Present Participle?

現在分詞由動詞加-ing構成，表達「主動」或「正在」的意味。一般充當形容詞的功能。

A present participle is formed by adding -ing to a verb, expressing "active" or "ongoing" meaning. It generally functions as an adjective.

例子 Example: The boring lecture made students sleepy. | 無聊的講座讓學生們昏昏欲睡。

例子 Example: The crying child needed attention. | 哭泣的孩子需要關注。

現在分詞與進行式 | Present Participles and Continuous Tenses

現在分詞的一種基本用法是與 be 一起形成動詞的各種進行式。

One basic use of present participles is with "be" to form various continuous tenses of verbs.

例子 Example: She is studying for her exam. | 她正在為考試學習。

例子 Example: They were working when I called. | 我打電話時他們正在工作。

注意：現在分詞作為形容詞使用時，與進行式中的現在分詞形式相同，但功能不同。

Note: When present participles are used as adjectives, they have the same form as in continuous tenses but serve different functions.

作補語 | As Complement

現在分詞作連綴動詞後的補語 | Present Participles as Complements after Linking Verbs

現在分詞可以在連綴動詞（如be動詞）後作主詞補語。

Present participles can function as subject complements after linking verbs (such as be verbs).

例子 Example: Some advertisements are misleading and cheating.

一些廣告具有誤導性和欺騙性。

例子 Example: The reason for the alcohol ban sounds convincing.

禁酒的理由聽起來很有說服力。

例子 Example: The movie was exciting from beginning to end.

這部電影從頭到尾都很精彩。

作修飾語 | As Modifier

現在分詞作名詞的修飾語 | Present Participles as Modifiers of Nouns

現在分詞可以放在名詞前或後，作為修飾語使用。

Present participles can be placed before or after nouns, functioning as modifiers.

例子 Example: English writing is a challenging task.

英語寫作是一項具有挑戰性的任務。

例子 Example: The crying baby woke the whole family up.

哭泣的嬰兒把全家人都吵醒了。

例子 Example: Employees working at home have fewer interruptions and distractions.

在家工作的員工受到較少的干擾和分心。

例子 Example: The man standing by the door is my brother.

站在門邊的那個人是我的兄弟。

作受詞補語 | As Object Complement

現在分詞作受詞補語 | Present Participles as Object Complements

現在分詞可以在某些動詞後作受詞補語。

Present participles can function as object complements after certain verbs.

例子 Example: People under great work stress find life frustrating and depressing.

工作壓力大的人覺得生活令人沮喪和壓抑。

例子 Example: The girl kept her boyfriend waiting for two hours.

那個女孩讓她的男朋友等了兩個小時。

例子 Example: I saw him running in the park.

我看到他在公園裡跑步。

例子 Example: We heard the children laughing outside.

我們聽到孩子們在外面笑。

現在分詞片語 | Participle Phrases

現在分詞片語作形容詞使用 | Present Participle Phrases as Adjectives

現在分詞片語可以作為形容詞使用，通常放在句首或句尾。

Present participle phrases can function as adjectives, usually placed at the beginning or end of a sentence.

例子 Example: Hearing the noise, she woke up.

聽到噪音，她醒了。

例子 Example: Facing high competition, people may suffer great pressure.

面對激烈的競爭，人們可能會承受巨大的壓力。

例子 Example: Being confronted with economic pressure, women have to go outside to work.

面對經濟壓力，女性不得不外出工作。

例子 Example: The man, feeling tired, decided to take a break.

那個男人感到疲倦，決定休息一下。

現在分詞片語的用法 | Uses of Present Participle Phrases

現在分詞片語可以表示時間、原因、條件、讓步等關係。

Present participle phrases can express relationships of time, reason, condition, concession, etc.

關係 Relationship 例子 Example
時間 Time Walking down the street, I met an old friend.
原因 Reason Feeling tired, he went to bed early.
條件 Condition Working hard, you will succeed.
讓步 Concession Knowing the truth, she still couldn't believe it.
