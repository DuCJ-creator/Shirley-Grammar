過去分詞介紹 | Past Participle Introduction

什麼是過去分詞？ | What is a Past Participle?

過去分詞表達「被動」或「完成」的意味。一般充當形容詞的功能。

A past participle expresses "passive" or "completed" meaning. It generally functions as an adjective.

例子 Example: The broken window needs to be fixed. | 破損的窗戶需要修理。

例子 Example: The excited children couldn't sleep. | 興奮的孩子們睡不著。

過去分詞的形式 | Past Participle Forms

過去分詞與完成式/被動語態 | Past Participles and Perfect Tenses/Passive Voice

過去分詞的一種基本用法是與 have/has/had 一起形成動詞的各種完成式，以及和 be 一起形成各種被動語態。

One basic use of past participles is with "have/has/had" to form various perfect tenses of verbs, and with "be" to form various passive voices.

完成式 Perfect Tense: I have finished my homework. | 我已經完成了作業。

被動語態 Passive Voice: The window was broken by the storm. | 窗戶被暴風雨打破了。

注意：過去分詞作為形容詞使用時，與完成式或被動語態中的過去分詞形式相同，但功能不同。

Note: When past participles are used as adjectives, they have the same form as in perfect tenses or passive voice but serve different functions.

作補語 | As Complement

過去分詞作連綴動詞後的補語 | Past Participles as Complements after Linking Verbs

過去分詞可以在連綴動詞（如be動詞）後作主詞補語。

Past participles can function as subject complements after linking verbs (such as be verbs).

例子 Example: I am well-prepared.

我準備充分。

例子 Example: Old people are usually more experienced and committed.

老年人通常更有經驗和責任感。

例子 Example: The old building appears abandoned.

這座舊建築看起來被廢棄了。

例子 Example: Her father felt worried.

她的父親感到擔憂。

作修飾語 | As Modifier

過去分詞作名詞的修飾語 | Past Participles as Modifiers of Nouns

過去分詞可以放在名詞前或後，作為修飾語使用。

Past participles can be placed before or after nouns, functioning as modifiers.

例子 Example: Retired people still can make great contributions to society.

退休人員仍然可以為社會做出巨大貢獻。

例子 Example: Practice is the only way to improve your spoken English.

練習是提高英語口語的唯一方法。

例子 Example: Boiled water is safer to drink than tap water.

開水比自來水更安全。

例子 Example: The books borrowed from the library must be returned on time.

從圖書館借來的書必須按時歸還。

作受詞補語 | As Object Complement

過去分詞作受詞補語 | Past Participles as Object Complements

過去分詞可以在某些動詞後作受詞補語。

Past participles can function as object complements after certain verbs.

例子 Example: She had her hair cut.

她理了髮。

例子 Example: The teacher found the student frustrated when he told her about the test result.

老師告訴學生考試結果時，發現他很沮喪。

例子 Example: You should have work and life balanced.

你應該平衡工作和生活。

例子 Example: I want this report finished by Friday.

我希望這份報告在星期五之前完成。

過去分詞片語 | Participle Phrases

過去分詞片語作形容詞/副詞使用 | Past Participle Phrases as Adjectives/Adverbs

過去分詞片語可以作為形容詞或副詞使用，通常放在句首或句尾。

Past participle phrases can function as adjectives or adverbs, usually placed at the beginning or end of a sentence.

例子 Example: Confronted with such a pressing unemployment problem, many experts bring up constructive solutions.

面對如此緊迫的就業問題，許多專家提出了建設性的解決方案。

例子 Example: Frightened by the thunder, the little girl ran to her mother.

被雷聲嚇到，小女孩跑向她的母親。

例子 Example: Built in the 18th century, the castle attracts many tourists.

這座建於18世紀的城堡吸引了許多遊客。

例子 Example: The man, exhausted from work, fell asleep immediately.

那個男人因工作而筋疲力盡，立即睡著了。

過去分詞片語的用法 | Uses of Past Participle Phrases

過去分詞片語可以表示時間、原因、條件、讓步等關係。

Past participle phrases can express relationships of time, reason, condition, concession, etc.

關係 Relationship 例子 Example
時間 Time Seen from a distance, the mountain looks beautiful.
原因 Reason Exhausted by the journey, they decided to rest.
條件 Condition Given more time, I could finish the project.
讓步 Concession Though warned about the danger, he still went ahead.

與現在分詞區別 | Present vs Past Participle

現在分詞與過去分詞的本質區別 | Essential Differences between Present and Past Participles

現在分詞和過去分詞作為形容詞時有本質區別：現在分詞表達「主動」或「正在」，而過去分詞表達「被動」或「完成」。

Present and past participles have essential differences when used as adjectives: present participles express "active" or "ongoing", while past participles express "passive" or "completed".

現在分詞 Present Participle 過去分詞 Past Participle 區別說明 Explanation
an interesting book
一本有趣的書		 an interested reader
一個感興趣的讀者		 書本身有趣 vs 讀者對書感興趣
a boring lecture
無聊的講座		 a bored student
感到無聊的學生		 講座本身無聊 vs 學生感到無聊
an exciting game
令人興奮的比賽		 an excited fan
興奮的粉絲		 比賽令人興奮 vs 粉絲感到興奮
a tiring job
累人的工作		 a tired worker
疲憊的工人		 工作使人疲勞 vs 工人感到疲勞

關鍵區別：現在分詞描述事物本身具有的特質（主動），而過去分詞描述人或事物受到影響後的狀態（被動）。

Key Difference: Present participles describe the inherent qualities of things (active), while past participles describe the state of people or things after being affected (passive).

更多對比例句 | More Comparison Examples

現在分詞 Present Participle: The surprising news made everyone happy.

令人驚訝的消息讓每個人都高興。

(消息本身令人驚訝)

過去分詞 Past Participle: The surprised audience applauded loudly.

感到驚訝的觀眾大聲鼓掌。

(觀眾感到驚訝)

現在分詞 Present Participle: The confusing instructions caused many problems.

令人困惑的說明導致了許多問題。

(說明本身令人困惑)

過去分詞 Past Participle: The confused students asked for clarification.

感到困惑的學生要求澄清。

(學生感到困惑)
