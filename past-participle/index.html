1. Definition 定義與形式

過去分詞 (Past Participle, 簡稱 p.p. 或 V3) 通常表示：

Completion (完成) / Passive (被動)

規則變化通常加 -ed，但不規則變化需要特別記憶 (如 go-went-gone)。

2. Perfect Tense 完成時態

與 Have 助動詞連用，表示「已經...」。

TenseStructureExample
Present Perfecthave/has + p.p.I have finished my work.
Past Perfecthad + p.p.She had left when I arrived.
Future Perfectwill have + p.p.I will have done it by tomorrow.

3. Passive Voice 被動語態

與 Be 動詞連用，表示「被...」。

Active (主動)

Somebody stole my bike.

(有人偷了我的腳踏車)

Passive (被動)

My bike was stolen.

(我的腳踏車被偷了)

Structure: Be (am/is/are/was/were) + p.p.

4. As Adjective (Passive/Emotion)

過去分詞可作形容詞，表示「被...的」或「感到...的」。

State / Condition (狀態)

  • a broken window (被打破的窗戶 = 破窗)
  • a closed door (關著的門)
  • frozen food (冷凍食品)

Emotion (感受)

V-ed (Past Participle)

人感到... (被影響)

I am bored.

(我覺得無聊)

V-ing (Present Participle)

事物令人... (主動給予)

The class is boring.

(課程令人無聊)

5. Passive Participle Clauses 被動構句

簡化被動語態的子句。省略連接詞與 be 動詞，保留 p.p.。通常表示「被...」。

Original: Because it was written in simple English, the book is easy to read.
Simplified: Written in simple English, the book is easy to read.
ExampleMeaning
Born in Taiwan, he loves bubble tea.(Because he was) born in Taiwan...
Seen from the sky, the island looks like a turtle.(When it is) seen from the sky...

6. Causative: Have/Get object done

表示「讓某事被做」（通常指花錢請人做，或遭遇某事）。

Structure

Have / Get + Object + p.p.

Example

I had my hair cut.

(我去剪頭髮了 - 請人剪)

He got his car fixed.

(他把車修好了 - 請人修)
