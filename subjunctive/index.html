The Realm of Possibility

Part IV: Subjunctive Mood · 想像與願望

The Unreal

表達與事實相反的假設。過去式表示現在的假設，過去完成式表示過去的假設。

  • Present Unreal: If I were... "If I were you, I would go."
  • Past Unreal: If I had known... "If I had known, I would have helped."

The Desire

使用 Wish 或 If only 來表達無法實現的願望或遺憾。

  • Regret: I wish I had... "I wish I had studied harder."
  • Desire: I wish I could... "If only I could fly."

The Mandate

在表示建議、命令、要求的動詞後，名詞子句需使用原形動詞 (省略 should)。

  • Suggest/Order: that he (should) do... "I suggest that he be present."
  • Adjectives: essential / vital "It is vital that she arrive on time."

Fact vs. Imagination

REALITY (FACT)
I am not a bird, so I cannot fly.
