虛擬語氣介紹 | Subjunctive Mood Introduction

什麼是虛擬語氣？ | What is Subjunctive Mood?

虛擬語氣是英語中的一種動詞形式，用來表達與事實相反、願望、建議、命令或假設的情況。它通常用於表達非真實的、想像的或希望發生的情境。

The subjunctive mood is a verb form in English used to express situations that are contrary to fact, wishes, suggestions, commands, or hypothetical situations. It is typically used to express unreal, imagined, or desired situations.

事實語氣 Indicative Mood: I am at home now.

虛擬語氣 Subjunctive Mood: I wish I were at home now.

If 條件句 | Conditional Sentences

If 條件句類型 | Types of Conditional Sentences

If 條件句是由兩部分組成的複雜句：一半是帶if的從屬條件子句，另一半是含情態助動詞的主要子句。

Conditional sentences consist of two parts: a subordinate clause with "if" and a main clause with a modal auxiliary verb.

類型 Type 結構 Structure 例句 Example 中文翻譯 Chinese Translation
Zero Conditional
表達基本事實		 主句和子句都用現在簡單式 If you heat ice, it melts. 把冰加熱，冰就會融化。
First Conditional
將來可能發生		 主句：將來簡單式
子句：現在簡單式		 You will miss the bus if you don't leave soon. 你再不走就趕不上巴士了。
Second Conditional
與將來事實相反		 主句：情態助動詞+動詞
子句：過去簡單式		 If I inherited a billion dollars, I would travel to the moon. 如果我繼承了10億元，我就會到月球去旅行。
Third Conditional
與過去事實相反		 主句：情態助動詞 + have done
子句：過去完成式 (had done)		 She would have gone to your birthday party if she hadn't been sick. 她如果沒有生病，就去你的生日會了。

注意: 當條件子句放在主句前時，句子之間需要用逗號。

Note: When the conditional clause comes before the main clause, a comma is needed between the sentences.

If 省略與倒裝 | If Omission and Inversion

在虛擬語氣中，if 可以被省略，此時需要將助動詞提前形成倒裝結構。

In subjunctive mood, "if" can be omitted, requiring the auxiliary verb to be moved forward to form an inversion structure.

正常語序 Normal Order: If I were you, I wouldn't get involved.

倒裝語序 Inverted Order: Were I you, I wouldn't get involved.

正常語序 Normal Order: If I had known, I would have offered help.

倒裝語序 Inverted Order: Had I known, I would have offered help.

正常語序 Normal Order: If you should have any questions, please email me.

倒裝語序 Inverted Order: Should you have any questions, please email me.

特殊結構 Special Structure: But for your help, we couldn't have succeeded in the experiment.

如果沒有你的幫助，我們的實驗不會成功的。

Wish 用法 | Usage of Wish

Wish 的用法 | Usage of Wish

Wish 用於表達與現在、將來或過去事實相反的願望。

Wish is used to express wishes contrary to present, future, or past facts.

Wish 的結構 | Wish Structure

現在：wish + 過去式

將來：wish + would/could + 動詞原形

過去：wish + 過去完成式

與現在相反 Contrary to Present: I wish I were home.

我希望我在家。（意思是：我現在不在家）

與將來相反 Contrary to Future: I wish it would rain tomorrow.

我希望明天下雨。（意思是：明天不會下雨）

與過去相反 Contrary to Past: I wish I could not have left my laptop in your home.

我希望我沒有把筆電落在你家。（意思是：我把筆電落在你家了）

Wish 與 Hope 的區別 | Difference between Wish and Hope

Wish 用於表達與事實相反的願望，而 Hope 用於表達可能實現的願望。

Wish is used to express wishes contrary to fact, while Hope is used to express wishes that may come true.

Wish (與事實相反): I wish I were taller.

我希望我更高。（事實：我不高）

Hope (可能實現): I hope you have a good trip.

我希望你旅途愉快。（可能實現）

Wish (與事實相反): I wish I had studied harder for the exam.

我希望我為考試更努力學習。（事實：我沒有努力學習）

Hope (可能實現): I hope you pass the exam.

我希望你通過考試。（可能實現）

特殊結構 | Special Structures

常見特殊結構表假設 | Common Special Structures for Hypothetical Situations

It's time 結構: It's time we went home.

是我們回家的時候了。

It's high time 結構: It's high time I bought a new computer.

是我買新電腦的時候了。（表達在時間上已經有一點晚了）

Would rather 結構: I would rather (that) you came another time.

我寧願你改天再來。

Would rather 結構: She'd rather (that) he weren't so angry.

她寧願他沒有這麼生氣。

注意: 當主句的主詞與子句的主詞不一樣時，子句用過去式。

Note: When the subject of the main clause is different from the subject of the subordinate clause, the subordinate clause uses past tense.

其他特殊結構 | Other Special Structures

As if / As though 結構: He talks as if he were an expert.

他說起話來好像他是專家一樣。

If only 結構: If only I had more time!

要是我有更多時間就好了！

But for 結構: But for your help, we couldn't have succeeded.

要不是你的幫助，我們不可能成功。

測驗練習 | Quiz Practice

虛擬語氣測驗 | Subjunctive Mood Quiz

測試您對虛擬語氣的掌握程度

Test your understanding of subjunctive mood

1. 如果我是你，我會更努力學習。
If I were you, I would study harder.
If I was you, I would study harder.
If I am you, I will study harder.
If I have been you, I would study harder.
2. 我希望我昨天沒有遲到。
I wish I am not late yesterday.
I wish I was not late yesterday.
I wish I hadn't been late yesterday.
I wish I wouldn't be late yesterday.
3. 老師建議我們應該每天練習英語。
The teacher suggests that we practice English every day.
The teacher suggests that we practiced English every day.
The teacher suggests that we (should) practice English every day.
The teacher suggests that we will practice English every day.
4. 是時候我們開始這個項目了。
It's time we start this project.
It's time we started this project.
It's time we will start this project.
It's time we are starting this project.
5. 如果昨天沒有下雨，我們就會去野餐了。
If it didn't rain yesterday, we would go for a picnic.
If it hadn't rained yesterday, we would have gone for a picnic.
If it doesn't rain yesterday, we would go for a picnic.
If it hadn't rained yesterday, we would go for a picnic.
