名詞子句介紹 | Noun Clauses Introduction

什麼是名詞子句？ | What is a Noun Clause?

名詞子句是一個具有名詞功能的子句，可以在句子中擔任主詞、受詞或補語的角色。

A noun clause is a clause that functions as a noun and can serve as the subject, object, or complement in a sentence.

例子 Example: What he said surprised everyone. | 他說的話讓每個人都感到驚訝。

例子 Example: I know where she lives. | 我知道她住在哪裡。

名詞子句的基本結構 | Basic Structure of Noun Clauses

連接詞 Connector 結構 Structure 例子 Example
that that + 完整句子 I believe that he is honest.
if/whether if/whether + 完整句子 I wonder if she will come.
疑問詞 what/when/where/why/how + 完整句子 I know what you mean.
疑問詞+ever whatever/whenever/etc. + 完整句子 Whatever you decide is fine.

作主詞 | As Subject

名詞子句作主詞 | Noun Clauses as Subjects

名詞子句可以在句子中擔任主詞的角色。

Noun clauses can function as the subject of a sentence.

例子 Example: What he said made me angry.

他說的話讓我很生氣。

例子 Example: Whether she comes or not doesn't matter.

她來不來都沒關係。

例子 Example: That he passed the exam surprised everyone.

他通過了考試讓每個人都感到驚訝。

例子 Example: Who will be the next president is still unknown.

誰會成為下一任總統仍然未知。

It 作形式主詞 | It as Formal Subject

當名詞子句作主詞時，常使用 It 作為形式主詞，將真正的名詞子句主詞放在句末。

When a noun clause is the subject, we often use "It" as a formal subject and place the actual noun clause subject at the end of the sentence.

例子 Example: It is obvious that he is lying.

很明顯他在說謊。

例子 Example: It doesn't matter what you think.

你怎麼想並不重要。

例子 Example: It is uncertain whether he will come.

他不確定是否會來。

作受詞 | As Object

名詞子句作動詞的受詞 | Noun Clauses as Objects of Verbs

名詞子句可以在及物動詞後作受詞。

Noun clauses can function as objects after transitive verbs.

例子 Example: I know that you are busy.

我知道你很忙。

例子 Example: She asked if I could help her.

她問我是否能幫助她。

例子 Example: He explained why he was late.

他解釋了為什麼遲到。

例子 Example: Can you tell me where the station is?

你能告訴我車站在哪裡嗎？

名詞子句作介系詞的受詞 | Noun Clauses as Objects of Prepositions

名詞子句可以在介系詞後作受詞。

Noun clauses can function as objects after prepositions.

例子 Example: She is thinking about what she should do.

她正在考慮她應該做什麼。

例子 Example: It depends on whether it rains or not.

這取決於是否下雨。

例子 Example: He is worried about how he will pass the exam.

他擔心如何通過考試。

作補語 | As Complement

名詞子句作主詞補語 | Noun Clauses as Subject Complements

名詞子句可以在連綴動詞後作主詞補語。

Noun clauses can function as subject complements after linking verbs.

例子 Example: The problem is that we don't have enough time.

問題是我們沒有足夠的時間。

例子 Example: My question is whether you agree or not.

我的問題是你是否同意。

例子 Example: The truth is that I don't know the answer.

事實是我不知道答案。

例子 Example: This is what I want.

這就是我想要的。

名詞子句作形容詞補語 | Noun Clauses as Adjective Complements

名詞子句可以在某些形容詞後作補語。

Noun clauses can function as complements after certain adjectives.

例子 Example: I am sure that he will come.

我確信他會來。

例子 Example: She is happy that you passed the exam.

她很高興你通過了考試。

例子 Example: He is afraid that he might fail.

他擔心他可能會失敗。

連接詞 | Connectors

名詞子句的連接詞 | Connectors for Noun Clauses

名詞子句由各種連接詞引導，這些連接詞決定了子句的類型和意義。

Noun clauses are introduced by various connectors that determine the type and meaning of the clause.

連接詞 Connector 功能 Function 例子 Example
that 陳述事實 I know that he is honest.
if/whether 表示疑問或不確定 I wonder if she will come.
what 事物、事情 I understand what you mean.
who 人（主格） I know who did it.
whom 人（受格） I know whom you invited.
whose 所有格 I know whose book this is.
which 選擇 I know which one you prefer.
when 時間 I remember when we met.
where 地點 I know where he lives.
why 原因 I understand why you're angry.
how 方式 I know how to do it.

連接詞的特殊用法 | Special Uses of Connectors

that 的省略：在口語中，that 常被省略，特別是在動詞後。

Omission of "that": In spoken English, "that" is often omitted, especially after verbs.

I think (that) he is right. | 我認為他是對的。

if vs whether：if 和 whether 都表示"是否"，但 whether 更正式，且可以用在介系詞後。

if vs whether: Both "if" and "whether" mean "是否", but "whether" is more formal and can be used after prepositions.

It depends on whether he agrees. (正確) | It depends on if he agrees. (較不正式)

特殊句型 | Special Patterns

名詞子句的特殊句型 | Special Patterns with Noun Clauses

名詞子句有一些特殊的句型結構，需要特別注意。

Noun clauses have some special sentence patterns that require special attention.

間接問句 Indirect Questions：

Where does she live? (直接問句) → I wonder where she lives. (間接問句)

她住在哪裡？ → 我想知道她住在哪裡。

注意：間接問句中主詞和動詞的順序與直述句相同，不需要助動詞do/does/did。

虛主詞 It：

It is important that you study hard.

你努力學習是很重要的。

同位語 Appositives：

The fact that he is guilty is obvious.

他有罪的事實是顯而易見的。

疑問詞 + ever：

Whatever you decide is fine with me.

無論你決定什麼，我都沒問題。

Whoever wins the game will get a prize.

無論誰贏得比賽，都會得到獎品。

常見錯誤 | Common Mistakes

錯誤： I don't know where is the station.

正確： I don't know where the station is.

我不知道車站在哪裡。

錯誤： She asked me that could I help her.

正確： She asked me if I could help her.

她問我是否能幫助她。

錯誤： I'm not sure that he will come or not.

正確： I'm not sure whether he will come or not.

我不確定他是否會來。
