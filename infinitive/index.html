不定詞介紹 | Infinitive Introduction

什麼是不定詞？ | What is an Infinitive?

不定詞是動詞的一種形式，通常由 "to" 加上動詞原形構成，在句子中可以充當名詞、形容詞或副詞。

An infinitive is a form of verb, usually consisting of "to" plus the base form of the verb, which can function as a noun, adjective, or adverb in a sentence.

例子 Example: I want to learn English. | 我想學習英語。

例子 Example: She needs a book to read. | 她需要一本書來讀。

不定詞的形式 | Infinitive Forms

動詞 Verb 不定詞 Infinitive 例子 Example
work to work I plan to work from home.
study to study She needs to study harder.
travel to travel They hope to travel abroad.
learn to learn We want to learn new skills.

作主詞 | As Subject

不定詞作主詞 | Infinitives as Subjects

不定詞可以在句子中擔任主詞的角色。

Infinitives can function as the subject of a sentence.

例子 Example: To work from home is one of her dreams.

在家工作是他的夢想之一。

例子 Example: To see is to believe.

眼見為實。

特殊句型：It 為虛構主詞 | Special Pattern: It as Dummy Subject

當不定詞作主詞時，可以使用 It 作為形式主詞，將真正的不定詞主詞放在句末。

When an infinitive is the subject, we can use "It" as a dummy subject and place the actual infinitive subject at the end of the sentence.

例子 Example: It is significant to create a secure home environment for our children.

為我們的孩子創造一個安全的家庭環境是很重要的。

與動名詞區別 | Gerund vs Infinitive

動名詞與不定詞的區別 | Differences between Gerunds and Infinitives

有些動詞同時可以接動名詞和不定詞，但所表達的意思不一樣。

Some verbs can be followed by both gerunds and infinitives, but they express different meanings.

動詞 Verb 接不定詞 + Infinitive 接動名詞 + Gerund
remember 記得要做某事
I remembered to lock the door.		 記得做過某事
I remember locking the door.
forget 忘記要做某事
I forgot to call him.		 忘記做過某事
I'll never forget meeting her.
stop 停下來去做另一件事
He stopped to buy a coffee.		 停止做某事
He stopped smoking.
try 嘗試/努力做某事
I tried to open the window.		 試驗做某事
I tried opening the window.
regret 對要做的事情感到遺憾
We regret to inform you...		 對做了的事情感到後悔
I regret sending the email.

例子 Example: We regret to inform you that your visa application has been declined.

我們遺憾地通知您，您的簽證申請已被拒絕。

(對要做的事情感到遺憾)

例子 Example: I regret sending the email to him.

我後悔發了那封郵件給他。

(對做了的事情感到後悔)

注意：類似還有remember, forget, stop, try 等，一定在平時的英文學習中理解積累。

Note: Similarly for remember, forget, stop, try, etc., be sure to understand and accumulate them in your daily English learning.

作補語 | As Complement

不定詞作連綴動詞後的補語 | Infinitives as Complements after Linking Verbs

不定詞可以在連綴動詞（如be動詞）後作主詞補語。

Infinitives can function as subject complements after linking verbs (such as be verbs).

例子 Example: The governments' obligation is to remove poverty, illiteracy and diseases.

政府的義務是消除貧困、文盲和疾病。

例子 Example: The most effective solution is to enhance the public's awareness of the rubbish problem.

最有效的解決方案是提高公眾對垃圾問題的認識。

省略to的情況 | Omission of "To"

在某些結構中，不定詞的"to"可以省略。

In some structures, the "to" of the infinitive can be omitted.

例子 Example: All you have to do is change your attitude.

你所要做的就是改變你的態度。

例子 Example: The only thing to do is make a lot of money.

唯一要做的事就是賺很多錢。

例子 Example: What the elders should do is enjoy their late years.

老年人應該做的是享受他們的晚年。

例子 Example: The best thing you can do now is write her an apology.

你現在能做的最好的事情就是給她寫一封道歉信。

作限定詞 | As Modifier

不定詞作名詞的限定詞 | Infinitives as Modifiers of Nouns

不定詞可以放在名詞後，作為限定詞使用。

Infinitives can be placed after nouns, functioning as modifiers.

例子 Example: We have the ability to fight against the virus.

我們有能力對抗病毒。

例子 Example: He got the idea to ask help from the teacher.

他想到向老師求助的主意。

例子 Example: The office is not a place to talk loudly.

辦公室不是大聲說話的地方。

不定詞作受詞補語 | Infinitives as Object Complements

不定詞可以在某些動詞後作受詞補語。

Infinitives can function as object complements after certain verbs.

例子 Example: The payment allowed him to buy a new phone.

這筆付款讓他能夠買一部新手機。

例子 Example: The teacher encourages the students to work harder.

老師鼓勵學生更加努力學習。

例子 Example: Jack's mother forced him to have his hair cut.

傑克的母親強迫他理髮。

例子 Example: The teacher helps us (to) practice writing.

老師幫助我們練習寫作。

使役動詞後的不定詞補語 | Infinitives after Causative Verbs

使役動詞(let, make, have)後的不定詞補語省略to。

The "to" is omitted in infinitive complements after causative verbs (let, make, have).

例子 Example: Let's play basketball.

我們打籃球吧。

(不是 to play)

例子 Example: The new policy made him attend the training course.

新政策迫使他參加培訓課程。

例子 Example: I regularly have the dentist check my teeth.

我定期讓牙醫檢查我的牙齒。

不定詞的否定式 | Negative Form of Infinitives

不定詞的否定式是在to前加not。

The negative form of infinitives is formed by adding "not" before "to".

例子 Example: I decided not to buy the new phone.

我決定不買新手機。
