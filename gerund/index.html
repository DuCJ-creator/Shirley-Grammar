動名詞介紹 | Gerund Introduction

什麼是動名詞？ | What is a Gerund?

動名詞由動詞加-ing構成，在句子中扮演名詞的角色，具有名詞的功能。

A gerund is formed by adding -ing to a verb and functions as a noun in a sentence.

例子 Example: Swimming is good exercise. | 游泳是很好的運動。

例子 Example: I enjoy reading. | 我喜歡閱讀。

動名詞的形式 | Gerund Forms

動詞 Verb 動名詞 Gerund 例子 Example
work working Working from home is common now.
read reading Reading helps you learn new things.
swim swimming Swimming is my favorite sport.
study studying Studying English requires practice.

作主詞 | As Subject

動名詞作主詞 | Gerunds as Subjects

動名詞可以在句子中擔任主詞的角色。

Gerunds can function as the subject of a sentence.

例子 Example: Working from home isolates employees from the corporate culture.

指「在家上班」這件事情，而非誰正在在家上班。

Refers to "working from home" as a concept, not who is currently working from home.

例子 Example: Reading extensively helps you learn vocabulary and grammar.

廣泛閱讀有助於你學習詞彙和語法。

特殊句型：It 為虛構主詞 | Special Pattern: It as Dummy Subject

當動名詞作主詞時，可以使用 It 作為形式主詞，將真正的動名詞主詞放在句末。

When a gerund is the subject, we can use "It" as a dummy subject and place the actual gerund subject at the end of the sentence.

例子 Example: It is simply a waste of time playing video games.

玩電子遊戲簡直是浪費時間。

例子 Example: It's no use crying after you fail a test.

考試失敗後哭泣是沒有用的。

介系詞受詞 | After Prepositions

動名詞作介系詞的受詞 | Gerunds as Objects of Prepositions

動名詞經常出現在介系詞後面，作為介系詞的受詞。

Gerunds often appear after prepositions, serving as the object of the preposition.

例子 Example: I am looking forward to attending the party.

我期待參加派對。

注意: 這裡的 "to" 是介系詞，不是不定詞符號

Note: "to" here is a preposition, not the infinitive marker

例子 Example: Many workers are opposed to increasing taxes.

許多工人反對增加稅收。

例子 Example: She is committed to studying abroad.

她致力於出國留學。

常見接動名詞的介系詞片語 | Common Prepositional Phrases with Gerunds

片語 Phrase 意思 Meaning 例子 Example
look forward to 期待 I look forward to seeing you.
be opposed to 反對 He is opposed to changing the plan.
be committed to 致力於 She is committed to improving her skills.
be used to 習慣於 I am used to working late.
be accustomed to 習慣於 He is accustomed to living alone.

作補語 | As Complement

動名詞作連綴動詞後的補語 | Gerunds as Complements after Linking Verbs

動名詞可以在連綴動詞（如be動詞）後作主詞補語。

Gerunds can function as subject complements after linking verbs (such as be verbs).

例子 Example: The top task of many companies is offering high salaries.

許多公司的首要任務是提供高薪。

例子 Example: The main reason for job-hopping is taking up new challenges.

跳槽的主要原因是接受新挑戰。

注意：在這裡，be + v-ing 並非現在進行式，而是動名詞作補語。

Note: Here, be + v-ing is not the present continuous tense, but a gerund functioning as a complement.

作限定詞 | As Modifier

動名詞作名詞前的限定詞 | Gerunds as Modifiers before Nouns

動名詞可以放在名詞前，作為限定詞使用。

Gerunds can be placed before nouns, functioning as modifiers.

例子 Example: swimming pool

游泳池

例子 Example: drinking water

飲用水

例子 Example: working experience

工作經驗

例子 Example: dancing hall

舞廳

例子 Example: dining room

餐廳
